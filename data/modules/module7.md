### The Closing Process

     - **Budget / amount**  
     - **Start date** and approximate timeline

   - Be responsive and patient‚Äîthis is normal.  
   - Stay flexible on small tweaks but protect:

     - Your brand and mission integrity  
     - Fair payment timing

4. **Execute & Invoice**

   - Once signed, send the **invoice** according to agreed terms.  
   - Immediately start the **onboarding process** so they feel momentum.

## 4. Nurturing Long-Term Partnerships

Sponsors stay when they feel **seen, informed, and proud** of the partnership.

#### Monthly

#### Quarterly

- More formal impact report  
- Short check-in call or email  
- Feature them on social media or newsletter  

#### Annually

- Year-end comprehensive report  
- Renewal conversation  
- Planning discussion for next year‚Äôs opportunities

### Going Above & Beyond

Small, thoughtful gestures make a big difference:

- Handwritten thank-you notes at milestones

  - Great photos  
  - Positive stories they can forward

## 5. Renewal Strategy

Don‚Äôt wait until the last week of the contract to talk renewal. Work backward from the end date.

### 90 Days Before Expiration

- Start renewal conversations  
- Share early year-end highlights and data  
- Ask about their satisfaction and upcoming goals  

### 60 Days Before

- Present a **renewal proposal**  
- Offer:
  - Upgrade options (higher tier, more stations, added benefits)  
  - Multi-year discounts or bonuses  
- Emphasize **continuity of impact** and momentum  

### 30 Days Before

- Follow up on the proposal  
- Address any remaining questions or concerns  
- If hesitant, suggest:
  - Staying at current level rather than dropping  
  - A shorter renewal period or pilot extension  

### Renewal Success Factors

Renewals are much easier when:

- ‚úÖ You delivered what you promised  
- ‚úÖ You communicated regularly (no long silent gaps)  
- ‚úÖ You provided **measurable impact data**  
- ‚úÖ You built genuine, positive relationships
