## 6. Creating Custom Packages

Standard tiers are your **baseline**, but customization helps close larger or more specific sponsors.

### When to Customize

- Company has **specific budget constraints**  
- They want **benefits from multiple tiers**  
- They have unique **activation ideas**  
- Very large potential investment (e.g., > \$25K)  
- Talks around **multi-year commitments**

### Customization Ideas

- **Geographic focus:** concentrate stations near their offices/stores  
- **Themed campaigns:** Earth Day, product launches, anniversaries  
- **Employee programs:** quarterly volunteer days, step challenges, internal competitions  
- **Content creation:** case studies, video stories, social media campaigns featuring the sponsor
